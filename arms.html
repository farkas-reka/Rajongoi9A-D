Arm Training

Arm muscle training is an essential part of a balanced fitness routine. The arms are made up of several major muscle groups that work together to support everyday movements and athletic performance. Strengthening these muscles improves arm strength and endurance, enhances upper-body definition, and reduces the risk of injury, particularly in the elbows and shoulders.

Most Famous Arm Exercises

Effective exercises for strength & recovery. No hype. Just proven back training.

Tricep Exercises

Dips
  • Targets: Triceps (all three heads), front delts
  • Why it's famous: Powerful bodyweight arm builder
  • Adds mass and pressing strength
  • Leaning forward = more chest, upright = more triceps
  • Variations: Bench dips, parallel bar dips, weighted dips
Triceps Pushdowns
  • Targets: Triceps (especially lateral head)
  • Why it's famous: Great isolation for arm definition
  • Constant cable tension
  • Elbows tucked = maximum activation
  • Variations: Rope pushdown, straight bar pushdown, reverse-grip pushdown
Skull Crushers
  • Targets: Triceps (long head focus)
  • Why it's famous: Deep stretch/li>
  • Excellent for mass
  • Elbows steady = better activation
  • Variations: EZ-bar, dumbbell, incline skull crushers
Overhead Triceps Extension
  • Targets: Triceps (long head emphasis)
  • Why it's famous: Stretches long head fully
  • Great for overall arm size
  • Overhead position = max long head tension
  • Variations: Dumbbell, cable, rope
Kickbacks

  • Why it‚Äôs famous: Excellent finishing/isolation movement
  • Strong peak contraction
  • Keep upper arm parallel to floor ‚Äî don‚Äôt swing

Diamond Push-Ups
