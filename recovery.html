Recovery Protocol

Targeted Stretching & Mobility for Peak Performance

1. Upper Body Mobility

⏱ 60 SECONDS TOTAL

Thoracic Spine Extension

Fixes "hunchback" posture from heavy lifting or sitting.

  • Place foam roller under shoulder blades.
  • Support neck with hands.
  • Lean back over the roller, exhaling deeply.
Focus: No lower back arching. Movement comes from the mid-back only.
⏱ 45 SEC / SIDE

Wall Chest & Bicep Stretch

Crucial after bench press or push-up sessions.

  • Forearm against a wall or doorframe.
  • Step forward with the same leg.
  • Rotate your torso away from the wall.

2. Lower Body Recovery

⏱ 90 SEC / SIDE

Couch Stretch

Intense stretch for quads and hip flexors.

  • One knee against a wall, other foot forward in lunge.
  • Squeeze glutes and stay upright.
Warning: Don't force the upright position if it hurts. Start slow.
⏱ 60 SEC / SIDE

90/90 Hip Flow

Improves internal and external hip rotation.

  • Sit with both knees at 90 degrees on floor.
  • Lean chest over the front knee.
  • Switch sides slowly without hands.

3. Spine & Nervous System

⏱ 2-3 MINUTES

Decompression Hang

Gravity-based recovery for the entire spine.

  • Grab a pull-up bar, let body go completely limp.
  • Feel your vertebrae opening up.
Breathing: Use "Box Breathing" (4s in, 4s hold, 4s out) to trigger recovery.
